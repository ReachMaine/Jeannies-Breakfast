Start your day with a hearty breakfast.

After the sunrise from Cadillac Mountain.

Jeannie’s Great Maine Breakfast is located in downtown Bar Harbor, Maine, just minutes from Acadia National Park.

Open every day at sunrise

Many of our diners like to have breakfast with us after viewing the sunrise from Cadillac Mountain, or before going out hiking, kayaking or sight-seeing.

Homemade using fresh ingredients

At our restaurant, we use only the freshest ingredients and cook everything on-site, including our famous homemade oatmeal bread and strawberry rhubarb fruit spread. We use locally grown Maine blueberries in our famous blueberry pancakes and blueberry fruit sauce.

Vegan and gluten-free fare available

When you eat at Jeannie’s you’ll be able to make sure your breakfast meets your dietary restrictions. Our vegan oatmeal pancakes topped with walnuts provide a delicious, hearty and cholesterol-free option. We also offer delicious Gluten-Free pancakes and english muffins. We continue to work with our homemade recipes to expand our menu with gluten-free options.

A great Maine breakfast is a long standing tradition.

Great Maine Breakfast is proud to serve you a wholesome breakfast menu based on the down-home culinary traditions of Maine’s logging camps, boarding houses, and sporting camps. You’ll find we use only the freshest ingredients and cook everything on site, including our homemade oatmeal bread and strawberry rhubarb fruit spread. Whether you’re hiking, kayaking, sailing, site-seeing, or shopping, a Great Maine Breakfast is the perfect way to start your day.

For our customers with special dietary needs, we offer Vegan and Gluten Free breakfast choices.

Owners Bryon and Susan Saunders and staff look forward to serving you and your family the best home-cooked food around.
